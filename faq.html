How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

You need first to go to the HZ prin page, log into your account and then you can upload all necessary files you want to print. After choosing file(s) you can set additional settings and confirm your print request. Next, you go to the printer (they are on each floor), scan your card in it, choose printing option and you can enjoy your printed document..
