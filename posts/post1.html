Study Choice

Before deciding upon the future of my career, I have read the brochure/site very carefully and also I have attended to a lot of information sessions about life at HZ and especially the ICT programme. Although, I am not quite good at memorizing, I tend to remember the logic behind the information I am given, so, when I discovered computer science/informatics, I thought it was just the subject for me. Now, searching for a programme that matches my interests, I found ICT, which involves, in my view, programming applied in real problems with real solutions. Applying the code I am writing to a real situation gives me the opportunity to taste the future work environment while I am still studying. I think the gaming industry would be quite interesting for me. In general, I would like to see my code coming to life in a real situation, maybe even creating/administrating sites for big companies. During my studies, I expect to learn how to assess situation in a computer logic matter and to develop knowledge in more programming languages (right now I only know some general coding in C++).
