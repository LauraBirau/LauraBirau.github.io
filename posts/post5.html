Before starting to look up for a possible future company I would like to work for, I sat down and asked myself: What do I actually want? To work alone or in a team? To have more little projects or a big,main one? To work from home, on site or somewhere in between? A static or dynamic environment? Those might not seem like important questions, but they determine my future satisfaction. After all, a good salary and a big vacantion are not going to make me get out of bed.I decided that I'm looking for the following in a job (at least the first one):

  • friendly coworkers
  • flexible hours (if possible)
  • a hybrid schedule
  • an employer willing to help
