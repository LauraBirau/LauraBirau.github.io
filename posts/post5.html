Personal SWOT analysis

Q:What do you do best? What are your positive traits?

A:I am a logical and curious learner. This helps me accumulate a lot of information and have a systematic approach to any problem.

Q:How can you turn your strengths / weaknesses into opportunities?

A:I am actively working on my team communication. I believe having leading abilities helps me organize a project and my logical thinking makes an order in the tasks.

Q:s What personality traits may be holding you back in your studies / career?

A:I tend to take the responsibility/lead in all my projects. Therefore, people often take advantage of this and I always end up with a lot more work than my colleagues.

Q:What obstacles do you face to succeed as a student? Could any of your weaknesses prevent you from succeeding in your studies / career?

A:I sometimes overthink and underestimate my abilities. But I believe this works well with my tendency to continuously test my powers.
